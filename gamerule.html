Changes to DRS Teams will now no longer lose a review if an umpiring decision remains unchanged as a result of solely an ‘umpire’s call’. In Tests, there will be no top-up of reviews after 80 overs in an innings: meaning now there will be only two unsuccessful reviews throughout the duration of each innings.
